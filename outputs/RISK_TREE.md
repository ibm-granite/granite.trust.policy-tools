# Safety Policy v1.0 - Risk Tree

## 3. Private Information
*Requests for any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual including home addresses, phone number, bank accounts, biometric data, credit card information, and other personal information*

- **3.1** Private Information Personal Identifiable Information
  - Requests for Personally Identifiable Information that inquire data that can be used to directly or indirectly identify an individual including information such names, addresses, social security numbers
- **3.2** Private Information Medical Records
  - Requests for medical information about individuals or how to illegally acquire such data should be denied
- **3.3** Private Information Biometric Data
  - Requests for biometric information where the user tries to extract private information or requests instructions to circumvent biometric systems should be denied
- **3.4** Private Information Education Records
  - Requests for private information about education of individuals
- **3.5** Private Information Financial Records
  - Requests for private information about education of individuals should be denied
- **3.6** Private Information Life Information
  - Requests for private personal life information

## 10. Financial Crimes And Illegal Trading
*Requests aiming to obtain information to commit financial crimes such as money laundering, insider trading, stock market manipulation, corruption, insurance fraud, among others.*

- **10.1** Money Laundering
  - Requests to obtain information to conceal the origins of illegally obtained funds in the attempt to make them legitimate
- **10.2** General Financial Fraud
  - Requests to get information and recommendation to commit financial crimes, to cover up fraud or unjustifiably frame another person or entity
- **10.3** Tax Evasion
  - Requests to illegally evade taxes
- **10.4** Insider Trading
  - Requests to make money through insider trading
- **10.5** Stock Market Manipulation
  - Requests to get information on how to manipulate the stock market
- **10.6** Bribery
  - Requests to solicit or give briberies without being detected
- **10.7** Corruption
  - Requests to take advantage of corruption opportunities
- **10.8** Embezzlement
  - Request to illegally withhold assets for personal gain
- **10.9** Insurance Fraud
  - Request to claim insurance money or fake insurance policies

## 11. Cybersecurity Risks
*Requests that aim to generate content that may serve an attacker to carry cybersecurity attacks such as learning how to run known exploits, write span emails, or create cybersecurity incidents for critical applications such as the energy sector*

- **11.1** Spam Phishing Sextortion Campaigns
  - Request to generate spam, phishing, sextortion, social engineer content for malicious purposes
- **11.2** Cyber Security Exploits
  - Requests to generate, use software exploits or take advantage of software vulnerabilities to gain access to cybersecurity systems illegitimately
- **11.3** Cyberattacks On Energy Infrastructure
  - Requests to generate content that can be used to carry out cyberattacks on the energy sector or other critical infrastructure including attacks descriptions, endorsement, instructions or threats
